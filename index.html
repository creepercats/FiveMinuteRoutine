Five Minute Morning Routine

Which path are you going to take today?


You brush your hair and wash your face every morning to conquer each day. Do you groom your mind as well?

Take 5 minutes each morning to prime your mind for habit-change...


We do this by proactively engaging in tangible actions specific to our goals: reading inspiring quotes, reciting mantras, and writing down our goals.

So you can accomplish your dreams!

IF you had the power to change ONE THING about your life, about yourself, what would it be? And I'm not talking about some impossible Herculian effort. I'm just talking about ONE THING that you don't like about your life, ONE THING that you would fix, or stop doing, that would change your life, or even SAVE your life.

For a lot of people it's the same ONE THING. But for some people it's different. Only you know.

So close your eyes, sit back, and take a moment to think about that ONE THING. "If only I could change this ONE THING about myself, my life would become much better."

And now I want you to write down that ONE THING on the line above. It helps to be explicit, because that's how we will know for sure what the ONE THING is...
